* Jet Skiing: Rs. 600 - Rs. 1,200 (for a 10-15 minute ride)

Jet skiing in the Andaman Islands is a thrilling and popular water sport that offers adrenaline-pumping fun while exploring the islands’ beautiful turquoise waters. With calm, clear seas and scenic coastlines, the Andaman Islands are an ideal location for jet skiing.

Jet skiing in the Andaman Islands is a fun way to combine adventure with sightseeing, giving you a unique view of the islands from the sea while satisfying your need for speed!

Important Tips:

  • Ensure you wear appropriate clothing, such as swimwear or quick-dry attire.
  • Follow all instructions given by your guide to ensure safety.
  • If you're a beginner, don't hesitate to ask for assistance during the ride.
  • Always check the condition of the jet ski and the operator's credentials before starting.

Important Instructions Regarding Weather & Activity Closures:

The Andaman Islands are known for their natural beauty, and as such, our operations are subject to the prevailing weather and sea conditions. Your safety is our utmost priority.

Please Read Carefully:
  • 1. Weather Dependency: All water sports activities are highly dependent on weather and sea conditions. This includes factors like:
    ○ Wind Speed: Strong winds can make the sea rough and unsafe for activities.
    ○ Rainfall: Heavy rain can reduce visibility (especially for diving/snorkeling), affect comfort, and make sea travel difficult.
    ○ Tides & Currents: Extreme tides or strong currents can pose safety risks.
    ○ Monsoon Season (Typically June to September): During these months, the sea can be rough, and many activities, especially those involving deeper waters like Scuba Diving and Sea Walking, may be closed or have limited operations due to safety concerns and poor underwater visibility. Ferries between islands can also be affected.
  • 2. Operator Decisions: The decision to operate or cancel any water activity rests solely with the local operators and the Andaman & Nicobar Administration based on real-time weather assessments. Their judgment is final for your safety.
  • 3. This includes, but is not limited to, missed activities, additional accommodation costs, or changes to your itinerary.
  • 4. Refund/Reschedule Policy for Cancellations Beyond Our Control:
    ○ If an activity you have pre-booked is cancelled by the operator due to weather or operational reasons, we will endeavor to:
    ■ Reschedule the activity for a later time during your trip, if possible and if slots are available.
