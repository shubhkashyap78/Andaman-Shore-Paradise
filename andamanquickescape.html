Andaman Quick Escape (Port Blair & Havelock), 3 Nights / 4 Days

  • Andaman
  • Description
  • Included/Excluded
  • Tour Itinerary
Package Overview Price (Per Person, estimated for 2 adults sharing, 3-star equivalent hotels):
  • A. 4 Days / 3 Nights: ₹18990
  • B. 6 Days / 5 Nights: ₹29800
  • C. 8 Days / 7 Nights: ₹45,500
  • D. 9 Days / 8 Nights: ₹52,500

These are approximate ranges. Actual prices will vary based on seasonality (peak season is Nov-Feb), hotel choices, specific ferry class, and negotiation with ground operators.

Package Overview Price:
₹12,000 - ₹19,000 per person (based on 2 adults sharing)

Target Audience:
Short trip, budget-conscious, first-time visitors who want a glimpse of the best.

Included / Excluded

  • Accommodation (3N): 2N Port Blair, 1N Havelock
  • Ferry (Private): Port Blair - Havelock, Havelock - Port Blair
  • Private AC Cab transfers & sightseeing as per itinerary
  • Daily Breakfast (CP Plan)
  • All applicable hotel taxes
  • Photographer
  • Flights
  • Lunch/Dinner
  • Entry Tickets to Cellular Jail/Museums
  • Elephant Beach boat tickets
  • Personal Expenses

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival at Port Blair & Local Sightseeing
    • Arrive at Port Blair Airport (IXB). Transfer to hotel
    • Afternoon: Visit Cellular Jail (outside view) and Corbyn's Cove Beach.
    • Evening: Witness the Sound & Light Show at Cellular Jail.
    • Accommodation: Port Blair
  • Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock Island & Radhanagar Beach
    • Morning: Transfer to Phoenix Bay Jetty. Ferry to Havelock Island.
    • Transfer to hotel in Havelock.
    • Afternoon: Visit Radhanagar Beach for sunset.
    • Accommodation: Havelock
  • Day 3: Havelock (Elephant Beach Optional) & Return to Port Blair
    • Morning: Optional excursion to Elephant Beach (boat tickets extra, approx. ₹1,000-₹1,500 per person).
    • Afternoon: Ferry back to Port Blair. Transfer to hotel.
    • Accommodation: Port Blair
  • Day 4: Departure from Port Blair
    • Morning: Free for souvenir shopping or visit a local museum (Samudrika Naval Marine Museum/Anthropological Museum).
    • Transfer to Port Blair Airport.
